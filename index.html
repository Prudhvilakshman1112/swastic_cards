Craft & Legacy

A 20-Year Legacy of Elegance in Visakhapatnam

For two decades, Swastik Cards has stood as a beacon of luxury invitation design in Visakhapatnam. Located in the heart of Daba Gardens, we have helped thousands of families announce their celebrations with dignity and grandeur.

Our specialization spans from traditional hand-crafted cards using high-density screen printing to innovative modern designs featuring embedded music sound chips, acrylic engravings, and architectural pop-ups. We pride ourselves on custom tailoring each invite down to paper thickness, typography, and wax seal crests.

Artisan Detailing

Velvet linings, sandalwood sprays, gold borders, and customized wax seal stamping.

Expert Print Studio

Advanced foil stamping, offset color printing, laser die-cuts, and thermography.
